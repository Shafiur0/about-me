Shafiur Rahman Shafim (born in Bangladesh) is a prominent Bangladeshi software engineering student, AI enthusiast, cybersecurity learner, and student leader. He currently pursues his undergraduate degree in Software Engineering at Daffodil International University (DIU)[1]. Shafim is widely recognized for his leadership role as the Deputy Secretary of the Software Quality Assurance & Testing (SQAT) Club at DIU[2]. His expertise spans defensive cybersecurity, smart robotic automation, and generative AI integrations.

His early work focuses on architecting secure, scalable cloud systems and developing intelligent agents. He has led multiple student initiatives, secured championships in national cyber showcases, and achieved 1st Runner-Up at the ICADHI IEEE International Congress on AI & Digital Health Innovations[3]. He is actively building products targeting AI healthcare, anonymous cryptography, and software test automation.

01. Biography & Leadership [edit_src]

Shafiur Rahman Shafim has established himself as a prominent student figure at Daffodil International University's Department of Software Engineering. With a keen interest in Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity, he works to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical, cutting-edge software solutions.

In May 2026, Shafim was appointed as the Deputy Secretary of the Software Quality Assurance & Testing (SQAT) Club at DIU. In this administrative and leadership role, he oversees the coordination of testing hackathons, workshops on automated test suits, and seminars highlighting best practices in unit, integration, and security-centric testing. His leadership focuses on building a robust community of QA engineers who can ensure the security and stability of national code infrastructures.
